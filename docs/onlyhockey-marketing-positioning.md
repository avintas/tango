# OnlyHockey.com - Marketing Positioning & Messaging

## Recommended Positioning

**Primary Tagline:**
"OnlyHockey - The Ultimate Hockey Trivia Game"

**Subtagline/Supporting Text:**
"(with community features)" or "Plus shareable hockey content"

## Strategic Rationale

**Why Trivia is Primary:**

1. **Core Product** - Trivia is what Tango CMS produces; it's the primary content type
2. **Monetizable Service** - Trivia is the service that can be sold and monetized
3. **Clear Value Proposition** - "Ultimate Hockey Trivia Game" is immediately understandable
4. **SEO & Discovery** - People search for "hockey trivia game," not "shareable hockey content"
5. **Differentiation** - Positions OnlyHockey as THE destination for hockey trivia

**Shareables as Supporting Features:**

- Wisdom, greetings, and motivational messages enhance the trivia experience
- They're "also includes" features that add value but don't compete with the core value prop
- They support community engagement and sharing around trivia

## Homepage Messaging

### Hero Section (Above the Fold)

**Main Headline:**
"The Ultimate Hockey Trivia Game"

**Primary CTA:**
"Start Playing" or "Play Trivia Now" - Prominently featured with visual of a trivia question

**Secondary Line (Supporting Features):**
"Plus shareable hockey content: motivational quotes, H.U.G.s, and hockey wisdom"

**Alternative Hero Messaging:**
"Test Your Hockey Knowledge" (with trivia as the clear focus)

## Content Categories & Their Purpose

### Trivia Games

- **Purpose:** Primary engagement, main attraction
- **Types:** Multiple Choice, True/False, Who Am I
- **Display:** Featured prominently, "Start Trivia Game" CTA

### Shareables (Combined Category)

- **H.U.G.s (Greetings):** Digital hockey love shared
- **Motivational Quotes:** Inspirational hockey content
- **Wisdom:** Hockey musings and insights
- **Purpose:** Content meant to be shared with others
- **Display:** Secondary but clear presence

### Hockey Stories

- **Purpose:** Supporting content, secondary pillar
- **Display:** Less prominent than trivia

### E-commerce

- **Greeting Cards:** Extend the "shareable" concept into physical products
- **Digital Products:** Can tie to either trivia or shareables
- **Purpose:** Revenue generation, supporting both main pillars

## Key Messaging Principles

1. **Trivia IS the Product** - Trivia is not just a feature, it's THE product/service OnlyHockey provides
2. **"Ultimate" Positioning** - Position as the best, most comprehensive hockey trivia destination
3. **Shareables Enhance, Don't Compete** - Shareables are bonus features that enhance the trivia experience
4. **Clear Value Hierarchy** - Trivia (hero) ‚Üí Shareables (supporting) ‚Üí Stories (secondary) ‚Üí Shop (revenue)
5. **Monetization Focus** - Trivia is what can be sold as a service; other content supports that

## Implementation Notes for Tango CMS

When building features or content in Tango CMS that support OnlyHockey:

- **Trivia questions ARE the core product** - This is what Tango CMS produces and what OnlyHockey sells
- **Greetings, Wisdom, Motivational** are supporting content that enhances the trivia experience
- Content generation priority: Trivia first, then shareables
- Trivia should be the most prominent, accessible, and well-featured content type
- Shareables should feel like valuable additions, not distractions from the main value prop
- E-commerce should primarily support trivia (premium trivia packs, trivia subscriptions) with greeting cards as secondary offering
